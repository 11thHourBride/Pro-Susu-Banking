Frequently Asked Questions
How do I activate my plan?+
After signing in, go to Settings → Plan & Billing. Enter your payment reference and start date to activate your chosen plan.
How do I pay?+
Send payment via MoMo or bank transfer using your company name as the reference, then enter the transaction ID in the activation form.
Can I upgrade my plan?+
Yes. Upgrade at any time from Settings → Plan & Billing. Your new plan activates immediately.
What happens when my plan expires?+
You receive warnings 30 days before expiry. After expiry a renewal screen appears on login. Your data is never deleted.
